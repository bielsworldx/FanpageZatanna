Poderes



Magia

Zatanna is one of the most powerful magicians in the world, having a mastery over both mystical and cosmic forces and able to manipulate reality.

Although she usually performs her enchantments verbally, she is able to cast her spells by other means. She is powerful enough to be considered a "Sorceress Supreme".[

Cura

Zatanna can completely heal a person of their physical injuries.

An example of this is when she healed Batwoman after a traumatic fight with Azrael by simply saying "laeh".

Ocultismo

During her tutelage with Nick Necro, she along with John Constantine was taught black magic by the renown mage, a form of magic known for having a high cost to it's practitioners.

Zatanna is an expert magician with knowledge of innumerable spells and was taught by notable magicians Zatara and Nick Necro.

Alerta Cósmico

Cosmic Awareness is the power to perceive and understand the workings of the universe on a cosmic level.

More than just knowing that the multiverse outside of their own exists, characters who possess this power have a distinct connection to the world outside their own.

Logomancia

Zatanna is able to speak her spells backwards to initiate a supernatural effect.

Zatanna's natural backwards speech has shown to make the impossible possible and to have a connection to the Dark Multiverse Otherkind.
